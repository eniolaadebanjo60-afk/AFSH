Our Flagship Innovation
Flagship Innovation

The Noiler Bird

A key milestone in AFSH's R&D journey is the development of the Noiler — a hardy, dual-purpose breed designed specifically for the Nigerian and African market. Noiler birds are bred to perform in both backyard and commercial farming environments.

Noiler birds bridge the gap between backyard and commercial farming, providing smallholder farmers with sustainable income, improved nutrition, and enhanced food security. Through continuous innovation, we remain committed to transforming the agricultural landscape with practical and impactful solutions.

Dual-purpose: eggs and meat production
Adapted to Nigerian climate and conditions
Superior disease resistance
Excellent foraging ability — lowers feed costs
Ideal for smallholder and commercial farmers

What We Work On

Our R&D Focus Areas

Every area of our research is driven by one goal — to give African farmers better birds and better outcomes.

Genetics & Breeding

We continuously evaluate and improve our parent stock genetics to enhance productivity, disease resistance, and adaptability to local conditions.

Biosecurity & Disease Control

Our biosecurity protocols are benchmarked against international standards, ensuring our hatcheries remain free from disease and our chicks are healthy at dispatch.

Hatchery Technology

We invest in state-of-the-art incubation and hatching technology, continuously optimising settings to improve hatch rates and chick quality.

Nutrition & Feed Science

We research optimal feeding programmes for each breed at every growth stage — helping farmers maximise performance while minimising feed costs.

Climate Adaptation

Our breeds are developed and tested to thrive in tropical climates, reducing mortality rates and improving productivity for farmers across Nigeria and Africa.

Performance Monitoring

We track field performance data from our farmer network to continuously improve our products and provide better technical support and advisory services.

How We Do It

From Egg to Farm

Every chick that leaves our hatchery goes through a rigorous, science-backed process designed to maximise quality and performance.

01

Parent Stock Selection

We source parent flocks from world-class genetic suppliers, selecting only the highest-performing breeding stock for our hatcheries.

02

Egg Collection & Grading

Fertile eggs are collected, graded, and sanitised under strict hygiene protocols before being set in our precision-controlled incubators.

03

Incubation & Monitoring

Eggs are incubated under optimised temperature, humidity, and ventilation conditions, with daily candling and breakout analysis to track embryo development.

04

Hatching & Vaccination

Chicks are hatched, processed, vaccinated, and quality-checked before being packed for delivery — ensuring every bird arrives healthy and active.
